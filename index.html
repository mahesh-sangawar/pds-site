A little about me

Hi, My name is Parimiti. I was born in Indian Middle class freedom fighter's family in a small village Umred located near Nagpur Maharashtra. (Well its not a village anymore its rapidly growing into a town) When I was born not only my parents were happy to see an angel born to them but even my elder brother was so thrilled that he went to say everyone that "Humko Ladki Hui hai" and he stuck to these words and treated me like actually his child.

I was always inclined towards dancing, drawing, sports mostly everything other than studies. Everything went amazing till year 12 and then i had multiple desires for further studies like at one point i wanted to join defence, next moment i thought of pursuing journalism then i got attracted towards Architect field also by this time i was no more shy and innocent girl i had turned outspoken who was very enthusiastic about life and future plans which were ruined by the so called well wishers of my family by misguiding me and my family into wrong career path.Now being a daughter of 2 professors and sister of a doctor I was given only 3 options to choose from 1. Had a great start to my academic with unexpectedly good result and hence I thought of using my creative ideas into computer programming. Few unethical incidents happened with me in my college which I couldn't handle and went into depression and my counsellor encouraged me make use of my talent in creative activities and thats how I attempted a record on painting in the year 2012-2013 which i was successful in it and hence I am listed in Limca book of records for painting 512 calendars on patriotic theme in 24 hours.
